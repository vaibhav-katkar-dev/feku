The Psychology of "No Contact"

One of the most telling signs your ex misses you is their reaction to your silence. If you've gone "No Contact" and they suddenly start liking old photos or viewing your stories, it's a classic sign of curiosity and lingering attachment.

Subconscious Digital Signals

People leave digital footprints of their emotions. A sudden change in their posting frequency (either too much or too little), posting sad songs, or "thirst traps" are often subconscious attempts to get your attention without reaching out directly.

Rose-Tinted Glasses

Our brains are wired to forget pain faster than pleasure. behaviors known as "fading affect bias" mean that after a breakup, your ex is likely to remember the good times more vividly than the bad, leading to feelings of nostalgia and missing you.
