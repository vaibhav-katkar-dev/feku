The Power of Compound Interest

Einstein called compound interest the "eighth wonder of the world." The biggest factor in your future net worth isn't just how much you earn, but how early you start saving. Small consistent habits today snowball into massive wealth over 10-20 years.

Mindset of the Wealthy

Self-made millionaires often share specific traits: they live below their means, they prioritize investing over spending on status symbols, and they have multiple streams of income. Our calculator considers these behavioral factors, not just your current salary.

Inflation: The Silent Killer

Your future net worth number might look huge, but remember to account for inflation. A million dollars in 2045 won't buy what it does today. That's why investing in assets that outpace inflation (like stocks or real estate) is crucial.
