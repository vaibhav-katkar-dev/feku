In modern dating, "Red Flags" are the warning signs we look for. But have you ever stopped to ask... what is my red flag?

Do you reply too late? Do you talk about your ex too much? Our scanner analyzes your "aura" (randomly, let's be honest) to give you a hilarious diagnosis.

Why We Ignore Red Flags

When we like someone, our brain releases dopamine and oxytocin, which can literally suppress the critical thinking parts of our brain. This is why "love is blind." We rationalize bad behavior ("he's just stressed") to maintain the chemical high.

Common Toxic Traits

Beyond the jokes, real red flags include: Love bombing (too much too soon), gaslighting (making you doubt reality), and consistent disrespect of boundaries. Our scanner highlights the funnier, lighter versions of these traits.

The Green Flag Counterpart

For every red flag, look for a green one: consistent communication, ability to apologize, and emotional stability. A healthy relationship is a balance, not a minefield of flags!
