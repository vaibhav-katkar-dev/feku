The Johari Window

Psychologists use a model called the Johari Window to understand self-awareness. There is a part of you that is known to others but unknown to you. This tool plays with that concept to confirm your suspicions (or fears!).

The Big 5 Guidelines

Modern psychology uses the "Big 5" traits (OCEAN): Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism. While we use fun archetypes, they loosely map to these scientific valid measurements of human personality.

Nature vs Nurture

Your personality is a mix of your genetics (Nature) and your environment (Nurture). While core traits settle by age 30, you can always shift how you express them. An introvert can learn to be a great public speaker, even if it drains their battery.
